COVID-19

Coronavirus disease is a infectious disease caused by a new virus.


The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases, difficulty breathing.You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently avoiding touching your face and avoiding close contact.



How it spreads?

Coronavirus disease spreads primarily through the contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a person that has virus on it.
